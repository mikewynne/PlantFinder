Special Lists

 

These are lists that we have created to give people a quick entry into a useable list that is not overwhelming. They may or may not correspond to one or more checked filters.


Super 60
The "Super 60" is a list of some of the highest performing plants found on the SF Plant Finder. Super 60 contains common, relatively hardy, local native plants that can be used to create high quality wildlife habitat in San Francisco. Selecting plants among the Super 60 will help lead to project success, and all plants found on the website have the ability to create and support quality habitat in San Francisco.

 

Pollinator

This list includes plants, native and exotic, that will attract native pollinators. Check more filters if you want to refine your list even further.

 

Top Twenty

Like the Super 60, these plants make great choices for creating habitat for bees, birds and butterflies in the urban environment. Try starting with these plants if you want to create a colorful and sustainable native habitat garden.

 

Sandy Soil

These local native species are the best for San Francisco’s sandy soils. They thrive in the good drainage and will be very drought-tolerant once established.

 

Shady Clay

The moisture-holding capacity of a north-facing clayey slope is much different from a south-facing sandy slope. We developed this list of plants, which should survive well in this moister environment once established.
