City Lists

 

These include many habitat-friendly plants, but not all of the species are necessarily habitat plants. Click on these lists, and then you can use the filtering system to refine your list for habitat-friendly plants or other characteristics after starting with one of these "City List" categories.


Sidewalk Landscaping
These plants are recommended by San Francisco Public Works as drought tolerant and adaptable to a variety of microclimates and sidewalk conditions.

 

Stormwater Palette
Recommendations from the San Francisco PUC for complying with the Stormwater Management Ordinance

 

Street Tree List
Approved street tree list for San Francisco reviewed and adopted annually by the Urban Forestry Council (UFC)

 

Thrifty 150
Durable, low maintenance, water wise plants that are recommended by San Francisco Public Works to do well in the challenging urban environment of the city.
